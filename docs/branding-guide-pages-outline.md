## PAGE 8: TYPOGRAPHY SYSTEM

**Purpose**: Establish typographic hierarchy and usage

**Visual Elements**:
- **Type Specimen Display**: Each font shown at multiple sizes and weights
- **Hierarchy Diagram**: Visual representation of heading levels 1-6
- **Example Layouts**: Show typography in action (podcast episode titles, quotes, body text)

**Content**:
- **Primary Typeface**: **Arial Bold** (Logo & Headlines)
  - Used for: Logo, page titles, major headlines
  - Weights: Bold only
  - Character: Modern, confident, tech-forward

- **Secondary Typeface**: **Arial Regular** (Body & Subheads)
  - Used for: Body copy, captions, subheadings
  - Weights: Regular, Italic
  - Character: Clean, readable, accessible

- **Accent Typeface**: **Courier Bold** (Tech Elements)
  - Used for: Data callouts, technical details, code references
  - Weights: Bold only
  - Character: Monospaced, technical, precise

- **Type Hierarchy**:
  - H1: 72pt Arial Bold (Podcast episode titles)
  - H2: 48pt Arial Bold (Section headers)
  - H3: 36pt Arial Bold (Subsection headers)
  - Body: 16pt Arial Regular (Descriptions, captions)
  - Caption: 12pt Arial Regular (Small text, credits)
  - Tech: 24pt Courier Bold (Stats, data points)

- **Line Length & Spacing**:
  - Optimal reading width: 60-80 characters
  - Line height: 1.5x for body text
  - Paragraph spacing: 24pt

**Visual Treatment**: Type specimen sheet with live text examples, not lorem ipsum

## PAGE 9: IMAGERY GUIDELINES

**Purpose**: Define visual style for all podcast imagery

**Visual Elements**:
- **4-Image Grid** showcasing style pillars:
  1. **Portrait**: Black female educator, confident, tech setting
  2. **Group Shot**: Diverse team collaborating with AI tools
  3. **Action Shot**: Latino male teacher demonstrating tech to students
  4. **Detail Shot**: Close-up of hands interacting with holographic interface

**Content**:
- **Photography Style**:
  - **Subjects**: Exclusively Black and Latino educators, leaders, students
  - **Setting**: Modern, tech-forward environments (not dated classrooms)
  - **Mood**: Confident, dynamic, future-oriented (not stock-photo stiff)
  - **Lighting**: Dramatic with tech accents (blue, gold lighting effects)
  - **Composition**: Rule of thirds, leading lines, depth

- **Image Treatment**:
  - Slight color grade (enhanced navy/gold tones)
  - Tech overlays permitted (data viz, holograms, interfaces)
  - Never: Generic stock photos, white-dominant imagery, outdated tech

- **AI-Generated Images**:
  - All podcast imagery created via Nano Banana (Gemini 2.5 Flash Image)
  - Prompts must specify: "Black" or "Latino", "professional educator", specific age range
  - Tech elements: holographic interfaces, neural networks, futuristic learning environments

- **Do's**:
  - ✅ Show real teaching/learning moments
  - ✅ Feature diverse ages (20s to 50s)
  - ✅ Modern, high-tech environments
  - ✅ Authentic expressions and gestures
  - ✅ Balance individual portraits with group shots

- **Don'ts**:
  - ❌ Generic stock photos
  - ❌ Dated technology (overhead projectors, old computers)
  - ❌ Tokenized diversity (one person of color in all-white group)
  - ❌ Stiff, posed, unnatural situations
