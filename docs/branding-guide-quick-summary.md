## OVERALL APPROACH

Every page maintains:
- **Cultural Authenticity**: Exclusively features Black and Latino educators in all imagery
- **Tech-Forward Aesthetic**: Modern environments, holographic interfaces, futuristic elements
- **Madison Avenue Quality**: Premium design, sophisticated treatments, 300 DPI resolution
- **Brand Consistency**: Navy backgrounds, gold accents, electric blue highlights throughout
- **Accessibility**: WCAG AA compliant color contrasts, readable typography

All images generated via Nano Banana (Gemini 2.5 Flash Image) with prompts specifying race, age, profession, and setting. Each page must score ≥90/100 on validation rubric before approval.
