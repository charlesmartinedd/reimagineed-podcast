## ðŸ“Š 10-Point Quality Assessment Criteria

### 1. Visual Impact & Professional Polish (10 points)
- **10 pts:** Striking, memorable design that commands attention; flawless execution
- **8-9 pts:** Strong visual appeal with minor polish opportunities
- **6-7 pts:** Competent design but lacks distinctive character
- **4-5 pts:** Basic design with noticeable amateur elements
- **0-3 pts:** Unprofessional appearance; significant design flaws

### 2. Brand Consistency & Cohesion (10 points)
- **10 pts:** Perfect alignment across all brand elements (color, type, voice, imagery)
- **8-9 pts:** Strong consistency with 1-2 minor variations
- **6-7 pts:** Generally consistent but with noticeable inconsistencies
- **4-5 pts:** Inconsistent application of brand standards
- **0-3 pts:** Little to no cohesive brand identity

### 3. Strategic Clarity & Purpose (10 points)
- **10 pts:** Crystal-clear strategic intent; every element serves the brand story
- **8-9 pts:** Clear strategy with minor ambiguities
- **6-7 pts:** Strategic direction present but could be sharper
- **4-5 pts:** Vague or unclear strategic messaging
- **0-3 pts:** No discernible strategy or purpose

### 4. Cultural Authenticity & Representation (10 points)
- **10 pts:** Genuine, respectful representation of Black and Latino educators; culturally resonant
- **8-9 pts:** Authentic representation with minor opportunities for deeper cultural connection
- **6-7 pts:** Surface-level diversity without deep cultural understanding
- **4-5 pts:** Tokenistic or stereotypical representation
- **0-3 pts:** Culturally tone-deaf or absent representation

### 5. Typography & Readability (10 points)
- **10 pts:** Masterful type hierarchy; exceptional legibility; sophisticated font pairings
- **8-9 pts:** Strong typography with excellent readability
- **6-7 pts:** Adequate typography; readable but not distinctive
- **4-5 pts:** Poor font choices or readability issues
- **0-3 pts:** Illegible or unprofessional typography

### 6. Color Psychology & Application (10 points)
- **10 pts:** Sophisticated palette that evokes desired emotions; flawless color theory
- **8-9 pts:** Strong color strategy with minor application opportunities
- **6-7 pts:** Safe color choices without strategic depth
- **4-5 pts:** Poor color choices or clashing combinations
- **0-3 pts:** Amateur or inappropriate color use

### 7. Imagery & Visual Storytelling (10 points)
- **10 pts:** Compelling, authentic imagery that tells the brand story; professionally executed
- **8-9 pts:** Strong imagery with minor opportunities for deeper storytelling
- **6-7 pts:** Adequate imagery but lacks emotional resonance
- **4-5 pts:** Generic or stock-looking imagery
- **0-3 pts:** Poor quality or irrelevant imagery

### 8. Content Quality & Messaging (10 points)
- **10 pts:** Powerful, clear messaging; exceptional copywriting; perfect tone
- **8-9 pts:** Strong messaging with minor refinement opportunities
- **6-7 pts:** Clear but uninspiring copy
- **4-5 pts:** Weak or confusing messaging
- **0-3 pts:** Poor writing quality or unclear communication

### 9. Innovation & Differentiation (10 points)
- **10 pts:** Boldly original; stands apart from competition; memorable and distinctive
- **8-9 pts:** Fresh approach with strong differentiation
- **6-7 pts:** Competent but follows expected patterns
- **4-5 pts:** Generic; looks like everything else
- **0-3 pts:** Derivative or clichÃ©d

### 10. Production Quality & Technical Excellence (10 points)
- **10 pts:** Flawless technical execution; print/digital-ready; professional standards
- **8-9 pts:** High-quality production with minor technical details
- **6-7 pts:** Adequate production quality
- **4-5 pts:** Noticeable technical flaws
- **0-3 pts:** Poor production quality; unprofessional
